Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Sebha Time respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Data Collection

Sebha Time does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account or login is required to use the app.

2. Advertising

Sebha Time displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous advertising identifiers in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Sebha Time does not have access to or control over data collected by AdMob.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Offline Usage

Sebha Time works fully offline. All counters and progress are stored locally on the user’s device.

4. Data Sharing

Sebha Time does not share any user data with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

Sebha Time does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.
